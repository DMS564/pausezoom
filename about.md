Pause Zoom
Zoom in and out on the Pause Menu using your scroll wheel!
Controls
Scroll Up — Zoom in
Scroll Down — Zoom out
Click & Drag — Pan around when zoomed in
R — Reset zoom to default
Settings
Zoom Speed — Adjust how fast each scroll tick zooms
Max/Min Zoom — Set your zoom limits
Smooth Zoom — Toggle smooth animated zooming
Reset on Close — Automatically reset zoom when leaving the pause menu
Works on Geometry Dash 2.2081.
